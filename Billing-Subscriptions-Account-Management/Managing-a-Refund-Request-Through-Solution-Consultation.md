### **Managing a Refund Request Through Solution Consultation**

#### **Situation**

A customer purchased a WordPress.com Business plan but immediately requested a refund after believing WordPress.com could not support their existing **.com.au** domain or provide email using that domain. They wanted to downgrade to the Free plan while retaining their website content. During the conversation, they contacted their Australian domain registrar (Melbourne IT) and learned that their domain could remain with the registrar and simply connect to WordPress.com through DNS.

#### **Task**

Clarify how external domains and Professional Email work with WordPress.com, determine whether the customer's requirements could be met without transferring their domain, and help them make an informed decision about their subscription.

#### **Actions**

* Clarified the distinction between:

  * Domain registration.
  * Domain connection.
  * Professional Email.
  * WordPress.com account email.
* Confirmed that the customer did not need to transfer ownership of their **.com.au** domain to use it with WordPress.com.
* Guided the customer through connecting the externally registered domain using the appropriate DNS configuration.
* Explained why the existing Professional Email address associated with the included **.com** domain could not simply be renamed to use the **.com.au** domain.
* Provided documentation for connecting an externally registered domain to WordPress.com.
* Provided guidance on creating Professional Email after the external domain had been successfully connected.

#### **Result**

After understanding that their existing **.com.au** domain could continue working with WordPress.com without being transferred, the customer withdrew their refund request and decided to keep their Business plan. They proceeded with connecting their external domain and continued toward setting up Professional Email using their preferred domain, avoiding an unnecessary cancellation and remaining on the correct implementation path.

#### **Skills Demonstrated**

* Customer retention
* Domain and DNS guidance
* Professional Email configuration
* Product knowledge
* Technical communication
* Solution consulting
* Customer education
