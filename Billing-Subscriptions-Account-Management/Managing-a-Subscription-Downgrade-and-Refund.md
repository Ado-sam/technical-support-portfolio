## **Managing a Subscription Downgrade and Refund**

#### **Situation**

A customer migrated their website to Wix while continuing to use their domain registration and Professional Email through WordPress.com. After an unexpected annual hosting renewal, they requested a downgrade and refund but were concerned that canceling the hosting plan would also interrupt their domain registration and email services.

#### **Task**

Review the customer's subscriptions, determine the relationship between the hosting plan and the remaining services, process the refund request, and reassure the customer that their domain and email services would remain operational.

#### **Actions**

* Reviewed the customer's account history and active subscriptions.
* Verified the relationship between the hosting plan, domain registration, and Professional Email services.
* Confirmed the status of the customer's refund request.
* Explained how the hosting plan, domain registration, and Professional Email function independently.
* Communicated the refund process and expected timeline.
* Reassured the customer that canceling the hosting plan would not affect the domain registration or Professional Email subscription.
* Continued supporting the customer by discussing future migration to Google Workspace for email services.

#### **Result**

The customer's hosting plan was successfully refunded while their domain registration and Professional Email services remained active. By clarifying the relationship between the different subscriptions, the customer gained confidence to proceed with the downgrade and was able to plan their future migration to Google Workspace without disrupting their existing services.

#### **Skills Demonstrated**

* Subscription management
* Billing support
* Technical communication
* Product knowledge
