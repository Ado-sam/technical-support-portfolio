## **Handling a Sensitive Account Recovery Request**

#### **Situation**

A customer could not remember the email address associated with their WordPress.com account and requested that support disclose the account's registered email address so they could regain access.

#### **Task**

Assist the customer in recovering access to their account while prioritizing account security and complying with privacy requirements that prevent disclosure of sensitive account information.

#### **Actions**

* Reviewed the available account recovery options based on the customer's circumstances.
* Explained why WordPress.com cannot disclose registered account email addresses, even to someone claiming ownership, in order to protect customer privacy and account security.
* Guided the customer through the manual account recovery process using available identifying information.
* Encouraged the customer to submit any relevant ownership details that could assist with identity verification during manual review.
* Explained how the manual review process works and what to expect during the investigation.
* Provided information about privacy and content removal options should account recovery ultimately prove unsuccessful.

#### **Result**

The customer received a secure and structured path for recovering access without compromising account privacy or security. By following established identity verification procedures, the recovery process protected sensitive account information while giving the customer the best opportunity to regain access.

#### **Skills Demonstrated**

* Identity verification
* Security policy
* Privacy protection
* Sensitive communication
