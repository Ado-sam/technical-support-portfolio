## **Recovering an Accidentally Deleted Account**

#### **Situation**

A customer accidentally deleted both their WordPress.com account and website and contacted support while still within the limited recovery period before the deletion would become permanent.

#### **Task**

Identify whether recovery was still possible and provide the fastest path to restore both the customer's account and website before the recovery window expired.

#### **Actions**

* Confirmed that the account was still within the 30-day recovery window, making restoration possible.
* Directed the customer to the original account deletion email containing the account restoration link.
* Explained the complete process for restoring the deleted WordPress.com account.
* Shared the official recovery documentation to support the restoration process.
* Guided the customer through restoring the associated website after the account had been successfully recovered.
* Emphasized the recovery deadline so the customer understood the urgency and could avoid permanent data loss.

#### **Result**

The customer received a clear, step-by-step recovery plan while restoration was still possible, maximizing the likelihood of successfully recovering both the deleted account and website before permanent deletion occurred.

#### **Skills Demonstrated**

* Crisis support
* Account recovery
* Time-sensitive troubleshooting
* Customer guidance
