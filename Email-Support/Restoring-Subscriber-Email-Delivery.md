## **Restoring Subscriber Email Delivery**

#### **Situation**

A customer reported that Jetpack had marked a subscriber's email address as undeliverable, preventing subscription emails from being delivered. However, direct emails between the sender and recipient continued to work normally, creating uncertainty about whether the issue originated within WordPress.com.

#### **Task**

Determine why subscription emails were failing while direct emails remained functional, identify whether the issue originated within WordPress.com's email infrastructure or the recipient's email provider, and provide accurate guidance to the customer.

#### **Actions**

* Reviewed the previous investigation and existing troubleshooting to avoid repeating completed diagnostic steps.
* Examined the recipient's email domain as part of the deliverability investigation.
* Compared the behavior of automated subscription verification emails with manually sent emails.
* Explained the technical differences between standard email delivery and automated subscription verification messages.
* Determined that WordPress.com's email infrastructure was functioning correctly based on the available evidence.
* Recommended additional testing and alternative verification methods to help the customer continue the investigation with the recipient's email provider.

#### **Result**

The investigation established that the delivery failure originated with the recipient's email provider rejecting automated verification emails rather than a fault within WordPress.com's infrastructure. The customer gained a clear understanding of the root cause and avoided making unnecessary changes to their website or subscription settings.

* Email deliverability
* Root cause analysis
* Customer education
