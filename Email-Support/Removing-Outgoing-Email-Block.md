### **Removing Outgoing Email Block**

#### **Situation**

A business customer contacted WordPress.com after receiving a notification that their site's outgoing emails had been blocked. They wanted to confirm whether the notification was legitimate and ensure that critical business emails, such as order confirmations, password resets, and notifications, were not being affected.

#### **Task**

Verify the legitimacy of the notification, determine why the outgoing email service had been blocked, guide the customer through the required remediation steps, and coordinate the restoration of email functionality once the issue had been addressed.

#### **Actions**

* Verified that the notification received by the customer was legitimate.
* Investigated the cause of the outgoing email block and confirmed it resulted from an unusually high number of bounced emails.
* Identified spam user registrations as a likely contributor to the excessive bounce rate.
* Explained the cause of the block in clear, customer-friendly language.
* Guided the customer through the required remediation steps, including:

  * Adding CAPTCHA protection to registration forms.
  * Removing spam user accounts.
  * Reviewing and securing user registration settings.
* Recommended configuring Google Workspace with SMTP as a long-term solution to improve email reliability and reduce the likelihood of future delivery blocks.
* Confirmed that the customer had completed all required remediation steps.
* Coordinated with the engineering team to request a review and removal of the outgoing email block.
* Followed up internally until the review was completed and communicated the outcome to the customer.

#### **Result**

After confirming that the required remediation steps had been completed, the engineering team lifted the outgoing email block and restored the site's ability to send transactional emails, including order confirmations, password resets, and notifications. The customer also received preventative recommendations to improve long-term email deliverability by routing outgoing mail through Google Workspace via SMTP.

#### **Skills Demonstrated**

* Technical troubleshooting
* Email deliverability and spam mitigation
* Cross-functional collaboration with engineering teams
* Customer education
* Clear technical communication
* End-to-end case ownership
* Proactive preventative recommendations
