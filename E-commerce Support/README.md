# E-commerce Support

## Overview

This category contains real-world technical support case studies demonstrating experience resolving issues related to **online store functionality, payment processing, checkout experiences, product and order management, e-commerce integrations, and store performance**. The examples highlight technical troubleshooting, customer communication, systematic investigation, and solution-oriented support across a variety of customer scenarios.
