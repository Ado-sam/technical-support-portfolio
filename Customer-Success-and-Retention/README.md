# Customer Success & Retention

## Overview

This category contains real-world technical support case studies demonstrating experience resolving issues related to **customer onboarding, solution consulting, customer retention, product adoption, account optimization, and delivering outcomes that improve customer satisfaction and long-term success**. The examples highlight technical troubleshooting, customer communication, systematic investigation, and solution-oriented support across a variety of customer scenarios.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Customer relationship and expectation management
- Solution consulting and product guidance
- Customer retention and issue resolution
- Product adoption and account optimization
- Customer communication and proactive problem-solving

## Case Studies

| Case Study | Summary |
|------------|---------|
| **[Preserving a Long-Standing Website Through Recovery Planning](./Preserving-a-Long-Standing-Website-Through-Recovery-Planning.md)** | Turned a potentially lost 20-year website into a structured recovery effort by providing multiple recovery paths, setting clear expectations, and proactively guiding the customer toward preserving their content despite account recovery limitations. |
| **[Developing a Long-Term Domain and URL Strategy](./Developing-a-Long-Term-Domain-and-URL-Strategy.md)** | Strengthened long-term customer success by providing a future-proof domain and URL strategy that minimized business risk, supported SEO continuity, and enabled informed planning for sustainable website growth. |

| **[Driving Customer Adoption Through Guided Onboarding](./Driving-Customer-Adoption-Through-Guided-Onboarding.md)** | Increased customer retention by transforming a frustrated new user into an engaged customer through personalized onboarding, targeted product education, and progressive guidance that built confidence in the platform. |
| **[Preventing an Unnecessary Account Cancellation Through Customer Discovery](./Preventing-an-Unnecessary-Account-Cancellation-Through-Customer-Discovery.md)** | Reduced customer churn by uncovering the customer's underlying goals, preventing unnecessary deletion of multiple websites, and guiding them toward a less disruptive solution through proactive consultation. |
