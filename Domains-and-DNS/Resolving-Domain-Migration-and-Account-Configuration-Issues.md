## **Resolving Domain Migration & Account Configuration Issues**

#### **Situation**
A customer accidentally registered the wrong domain, transferred domains between multiple websites, upgraded and canceled hosting plans, and became uncertain about where their website content resided. The case had already been handled by several support engineers, requiring a comprehensive review before further action.

#### **Task**
Review the customer's account history, determine the intended website structure, align the correct domain with the appropriate website and hosting plan, and simplify the account configuration so the customer could continue building their site.

#### **Actions**
* Reviewed the customer's complete account history to understand the sequence of domain registrations, transfers, and hosting plan changes.
* Verified domain ownership, transfer status, and the current configuration of each website.
* Confirmed the customer's hosting plan history and identified an available free domain credit.
* Determined the customer's intended website structure and long-term publishing goals.
* Explained the difference between WordPress.com subdomains and purchased custom domains to clarify where website content was hosted.
* Restored the customer's available free domain credit.
* Guided the transfer of the purchased domain to the correct website.
* Assisted the customer with hosting plan changes and explained renewal timelines to prevent future confusion.
* Recommended appropriate themes based on the customer's goals and explained the platform's hosting and feature limitations.

#### **Result**
The customer's domain, hosting plan, and website configuration were successfully aligned, eliminating confusion caused by multiple domains, plan changes, and websites. The customer restored their free domain credit, transferred the correct domain to the intended site, removed unnecessary subscriptions, selected a new theme, and resumed building their website. They confirmed the domain transfer was successful and expressed appreciation for the support received.

#### **Skills Demonstrated**
* Domain management
* Site migration
* Billing guidance
* Cross-agent continuity
* Customer education
